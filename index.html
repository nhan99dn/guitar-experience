Notes

Most of the people who like to play guitar begin with learning notes and theory of music, which is quite boring but really promising and good for the future, because there is a level that do not require you to sharpen your skill on guitar but you have to understand why and how the music is going happen, and when you get comfort with that, you are ready for many new levels and stuff that really interesting.

To my knowledge, studying and learning the theory of music is the best way for the beginers to learn guitar, but it is not the only one for you to take initial with, but I guarantee for you guys that those are the good way to learning guitar

This is a hardest and most challenging part of learning how to play guitar because start to learning everything is always hard and you have to get comfort with it. Remember that every successful person begin to start with a basic things and they are people that failed a lot. So do not frustrated when you find something hard to learn, begin with small step and take time with that. As long as you comfortable and love that, you will do it unconditionally and you will love this instrument like I do : "They can do it, why we are not?"
